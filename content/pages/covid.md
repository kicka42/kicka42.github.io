The coronavirus pandemic has caused widespread panic and disruption across the world. As the virus continues to spread, it is important to stay up to date on the latest data and developments.

Tableau is a powerful data visualization tool that can help make sense of complex data sets. I used Tableau to create[ a series of visualizations](https://public.tableau.com/views/COVID-19Europe/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link) that examine the spread of COVID-19 across Europe.
